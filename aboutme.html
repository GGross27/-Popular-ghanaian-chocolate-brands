About Me

Background and Author's Note

Hello! My name is Gabrielle Gross. The inspiration for this website came from my half Ghanaian background and my love for international chocolate. I've always cherished the Ghanaian culture. I find their customs and clothing really beautiful and their food very delicous. Over the years I've tasted a few of these chocolate brands but there are still a few that I've honestly never heard of until I started reaserching for this website. This website was a learning experience for me and I hope you will learn something new from it as well. I hope you find this website interesting!
