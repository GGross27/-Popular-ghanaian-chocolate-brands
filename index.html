Popular Chocolate Brands That Are Made in Ghana

Ghanaian Chocolate - Overview

Ghana is known for being the second-largest cocoa-producing country in the world. It has garnered a reputation for producing very fine cocoa beans and high-quality chocolate. Check out this video for more inside information.

About These Brands

Here is a general explanation of the backgrounds of each of the brands.

Golden Tree

Background

Produced from 100% Ghanaian cocoa and no cocoa substitutes, Golden Tree chocolate bars are truly an exceptionally unique brand. The chocolate comes in 7 different flavors and is made to be rich and durable to withstand the high and hot temperatures in Ghana. Despite its firm and hard texture, the chocolate itself is very smooth and will eventually melt in your mouth.

Website link

goldentreeghana.com

Niche Chocolate

Background

Niche not only produces chocolate bars but also a plethora of semi-complete cocoa products which are exported all over the world. Niche has about a dozen chocolate bar flavors, each one more unique than the other. Despite a few of the flavor combinations sounding rather peculiar, one taste and you'll realize that they actually complement each other. Overall Niche produces good quality chocolate bars that comes in different flavors and sizes as well as different products that are used by other companies around the world.

Website link

delfiventuresgh.com

Fairafric

Background

Fairafric is a German-Ghanaian company that uses only local Ghanaian cocoa and other raw Ghanaian materials for their products. Their overall goal is to produce excellent quality chocolate that consumers can enjoy and is profitable to them and Africa as a whole.

Website link

Fairafric.com

57 chocolate

Background

57 Chocolate is a luxury chocolate company owned by black women. The number 57 in the brand name is a tribute to when Ghana gained its independence in 1957. The chocolates consist of very fine organic cocoa beans from Ghana.

Website link

57chocolategh.com

Midunu Chocolates

Background

Midunu Chocolates is a luxury chocolate brand that produces handmade chocolates. The chocolates consist of Ghanaian cocoa and the designs featured on the top of the chocolates are all designs inspired by African prints and art styles.

Website link

midunuchocolates.com

Bioko Treats

Background

Bioko Treats is a chocolate company located in Ghana. The company uses Forastero cocoa, found in the Eastern Region of Ghana, and other local ingredients to make its chocolates. They make milk, dark, and white chocolate which are packaged in boxes of 6, 12, and 24.

Website link

biokotreats.com

Adansi Bar Chocolate

Background

The Adansi Bar Chocolate is a product from the Adansi Sweet company. The is located in the Kumasi Ashanti Region of Ghana and uses Ghana cocoa in its chocolate bars and other cocoa products. Their chocolate can be custom-ordered as bars or a package of fancy chocolate truffle domes or squares.

Website link

adansisweetgh.com

Chocoluv Chocolates

Background

Chocoluv is a chocolate company that uses Ghanaian cocoa beans for its chocolate bars and other cocoa products. Their chocolate bars come in an assortment of flavors, including milk, dark, ginger, and coffee-flavored chocolate.

Website link

choco-luv.com

Kabi Chocolates

Background

Kabi is a chocolate company based in Accra, Ghana. The company used Ghanaian cocoa for its chocolate bars and other cocoa products. Their chocolate flavors include but are not limited to milk, dark, and white. The chocolate comes in regular bars, as baking chocolate, or can be custom ordered.
